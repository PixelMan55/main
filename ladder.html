How to play

Climb from the start word to the goal.
  1. Each move, change exactly one letter to form a new rung.
  2. Every rung must be a real word.
  3. Reach the goal word to complete the day's ladder.
  4. Come back daily to build your streak — miss a day and every streak resets.
  5. Normal mode and hard mode each keep their own streak. Hard mode turns off hints and undo.
